Our Story

Why Finaura™ Exists

"Every rupee you invest must have a purpose, a timeline, and a plan behind it."

Finaura™ was built out of a frustration that many of us in Ludhiana — and across India — know all too well. Families that work hard, earn decently, and yet somehow never seem to build real wealth. Not because they don't save, but because nobody ever sat down with them and built a proper plan.

The typical experience in Indian personal finance: a relative who sells insurance pushes you into a ULIP. A colleague tells you about a "hot" fund. Your bank's RM calls every quarter with a new product. And somewhere in between, you have 4–5 SIPs in random funds, two LIC policies you don't fully understand, and an FD you keep rolling over.

Finaura™ was started to fix that. We believe financial clarity isn't a luxury for the wealthy — it's something every salaried Indian family deserves. That's why we offer goal-based investment planning and goal-based financial planning to families across Ludhiana and Punjab.
