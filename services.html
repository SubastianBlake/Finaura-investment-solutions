Yes. Switching your distributor (ARN) has zero cost to you. Your NAV, units and returns remain unchanged. We handle all the paperwork. We also review your existing portfolio first.

For KYC: PAN card, Aadhaar card, cancelled cheque, and a passport photo. If you're already KYC-verified (most people are), you can start immediately. We guide you through every step.

All investments are held in your name on BSE Star MF / NSE Invest platforms. You get email confirmations for every transaction. We also provide quarterly portfolio summaries and are available on WhatsApp between reviews.

Short-term market falls are normal and expected in equity mutual funds. A goal-based SIP is designed to stay invested through volatility — this is actually when you accumulate more units at lower prices. We help you understand this before you invest, so you don't make panic decisions when markets dip.

All mutual fund investments allow you to add a nominee at the time of investing. In the event of your passing, the units are transferred to your nominee directly — no court process needed. We guide you through proper nomination during onboarding so your family is always protected.

As an AMFI-registered distributor (ARN-293004), Finaura™ earns a trail commission from the AMC — a small percentage of your invested amount paid by the fund house, not deducted from your returns. This is the standard distributor model regulated by SEBI and AMFI. You can verify our ARN on the AMFI website at any time.
